I am Associate Professor of Economics at Aalto University and Helsinki GSE, co-PI of the Education for the Future Flagship Center, and part-time Associate Research Professor at the VATT Institute for Economic Research. My research focuses on education, migration, and social mobility, sometimes in multidisciplinary collaborations.

I received my PhD from the Helsinki School of Economics in 2009. I have worked as a postdoctoral researcher at LSE and been a visiting scholar at MIT and UCL.
